tennis club

Tenez is one of the finest tennis clubs in India. It has nine courts, four of which are clay courts and five are synthetic hard courts. Three of the synthetic courts are roofed and lighted. The Club’s show court, court no 1, is clay and has flood lights. The overall infrastructure is world class, and it is a matter of pride that the TTC complex has, over the years, played host to several international and national tennis events.
